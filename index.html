Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I upload a screenshot?
    Open the app, tap the screenshot upload button to the left of the text input box, and select your image. The app will automatically read it and generate replies or explanations.
  • What are the different modes?
    You can rewrite, auto-reply, or explain in 5 styles: Drunk, Man, Woman, Funny, and Bigfoot.
  • Where is my data stored?
    Bigfoot Brain processes text securely via OpenAI’s API. We do not sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
